# RAGScaleGuard Trademark And Branding Policy

The RAGScaleGuard name, shield logo, cube/orbit mark, and "Reliable AI retrieval" lock-up identify the official RAGScaleGuard project.

The source code is open source, but that does not grant permission to misuse the RAGScaleGuard marks. Modified distributions, hosted copies, forks, packages, demos, and dashboards must not present themselves as the official RAGScaleGuard project unless they are maintained by the project owner or have written permission.

You may use the marks for truthful references to this project, unmodified redistribution of the official project, and compatibility statements that are not misleading.

You may not remove official attribution from redistributed copies, use the marks for a competing renamed product, or imply endorsement by RAGScaleGuard when none exists.

If you publish a modified fork, make the changes clear and use your own project name while retaining attribution to RAGScaleGuard where required.
